⛩ Read Before Applying ⛩
✦ Lore Actors are NOT OC players. An OC (Original Character) is a personal character you own and play as. A Lore Actor is a staff role — you will be given characters to portray in service of the story. You cannot hold both roles simultaneously.
✦ Lore Actors cannot be Kage. The Kage position is reserved for OC players who have earned it through roleplay progression. Lore Actors operate behind the scenes as side characters — not as village leaders.
✦ You will play multiple side characters. Your purpose is to progress and enrich the server's lore — NPCs, antagonists, messengers, merchants, missing-nin, and more. No single character is "yours" to keep.
✦ Lore Actors are expected to be available, reliable, and impartial. You serve the narrative, not personal gain. Abuse of the role will result in immediate removal.
